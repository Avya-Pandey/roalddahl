Roald Dahl







This is about me.

Roald Dahl is the greatest children`s author in the world. We know this because he has written so many children`s books during his lifetime. In total, he has written 20 books for children. It doesn`t stop there either; he has written countless short stories and books for adults too. This shows he was brilliant at what he did - writing books!


Roald Dahl is known to be the best children`s author because he has won so many awards. In 2013, Dahl came top of a survey by a book selling company. He beat authors such as J.K.Rowling, Enid Blyton and Beatrix Potter. In the poll, Dahl was voted as the top author chosen by children (he gained 51% of their votes). He also secured 47% of the adult votes and won top author voted by adults too. This could possibly be because the adults had read Dahl`s books when they were children too!


Everyone knows that Dahl has won countless awards throughout his lifetime, and beyond, for his writing. He won four awards across the world in 1983 alone, mainly for his novel The Witches. Some of the most prestigious awards he has achieved include the New York Times Outstanding Books Award and the Whitbread Award. I bet you didn`t know that!


Dahl is renowned as the best children`s author because his novels are so popular. The most well-liked books include Matilda, Fantastic Mr Fox and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. We all know his books are popular because many of them have been made into films and plays, which are all still loved today. Have you seen The BFG at the cinema? Many of his books have been translated into different languages too; now children across the world can enjoy his stories. He is not only popular in the UK, but across the world also. Surely you would agree that that would take some beating to be the best children`s author!


The fact that there is a museum all about Roald Dahl and his books in Buckinghamshire where he lived as an adult, also proves that he is a much-loved author. There you can see his original ideas and sketches based around the novels he wrote. Furthermore, you can even see where he wrote his books by visiting the reading room inside his house. It is well worth a visit!


Having read all of the evidence, there is no doubt you would agree that Roald Dahl is the best children`s author the world has seen. He has written so many books in his lifetime, has won a number of awards and is still voted the most loved children`s author today by children! I am sure that you are as big a fan as I am!
